Agricultural & Bioresources Engineering Library

What is Agricultural & Bioresources Engineering?

Agricultural & Bioresources Engineering is the fusion of technology with agriculture, focusing on sustainable food production, irrigation, machinery design, and environmental conservation. Students learn to apply engineering principles to solve real-world agricultural challenges.
